# 7 LinkedIn Post Types — MMIND.ai Content System

## Type 1: Awareness Post
**Goal:** New reach, bold claims, challenge assumptions
**Engagement:** High
**Length:** 1,200–1,800 characters

**Hook Pattern:**
- Provocative statement or surprising data point
- "Most [audience] believe [assumption]. The data says otherwise."
- Bold claim + proof

**Structure:**
1. Hook (2-3 lines): Bold claim or surprising statistic
2. Context: Why this matters now
3. Evidence: 2-3 supporting points with data
4. Reframe: What this means for the reader
5. CTA + Standard Footer

**Recommended Visual:** Stil A — Bold Data Statement (4:5 portrait, one big number)

---

## Type 2: Personal Story
**Goal:** Connection, trust, authenticity
**Engagement:** Medium
**Length:** 1,500–2,200 characters

**Hook Pattern:**
- "Last week, something happened that changed how I think about [topic]."
- Personal moment → universal lesson
- Vulnerability + expertise

**Structure:**
1. Hook: Personal moment or observation
2. Story: What happened (concrete, specific)
3. Turning point: The insight
4. Lesson: What it means for the reader
5. Reflection question + Standard Footer

**Recommended Visual:** Stil E — Flat Timeline / Minimal Quote Card

---

## Type 3: Education Post
**Goal:** Credibility, saves, shares
**Engagement:** High
**Length:** 2,000–2,800 characters

**Hook Pattern:**
- "Here's a framework that [specific outcome]:"
- "After [X] implementations, these [N] steps always work:"
- List/framework promise

**Structure:**
1. Hook: Promise of practical value
2. Context: Why this knowledge matters
3. Framework: 3-5 numbered steps (each: What + Why + Example)
4. Summary: Key takeaway in one sentence
5. Save CTA + Standard Footer

**Recommended Visual:** Stil B — Flat Cards Carousel (one card per step)

---

## Type 4: Conversion Post
**Goal:** Leads, direct CTA, pipeline
**Engagement:** Low (but high-intent)
**Length:** 2,500–3,500 characters

**Hook Pattern:**
- Problem the reader is experiencing right now
- "If you're [specific situation], this is for you."
- Pain point → solution preview

**Structure:**
1. Hook: Specific pain point the audience feels
2. Problem: Deep dive into consequences
3. Solution preview: What the outcome looks like
4. Social proof: Numbers, testimonials, case study
5. Direct CTA (calendar link, DM, reply) + Standard Footer

**Recommended Visual:** Stil D — Data Comparison (Before/After)

---

## Type 5: How We Do It
**Goal:** Methodology showcase, soft CTA
**Engagement:** Low
**Length:** 1,800–2,600 characters

**Hook Pattern:**
- "Here's exactly how we [specific process] at MMIND.ai:"
- Behind-the-scenes transparency
- Process revelation

**Structure:**
1. Hook: "Here's how we actually do [X]"
2. Context: Why we do it this way
3. Process: Step-by-step walkthrough (real tools, real numbers)
4. Result: What this produces
5. Soft CTA ("Curious how this works for your team?") + Standard Footer

**Recommended Visual:** Stil C — Structured Infographic (process flow)

---

## Type 6: Building In Public
**Goal:** Transparency, community, trust
**Engagement:** Medium
**Length:** 1,200–1,800 characters

**Hook Pattern:**
- "Week [X] of building [project]. Here's what happened:"
- Real numbers, real challenges
- Honest update

**Structure:**
1. Hook: Update with specific timeframe
2. What we built/shipped this week
3. What didn't work (honesty)
4. What's next
5. Question for the audience + Standard Footer

**Recommended Visual:** Data Infographic (metrics, progress bars)

---

## Type 7: Lead Magnet
**Goal:** Email list growth, resource distribution
**Engagement:** High
**Length:** 1,500–2,000 characters

**Hook Pattern:**
- "I built [resource] that [specific outcome]. Free for you:"
- Value-first, then ask
- Resource preview

**Structure:**
1. Hook: What the resource does (specific outcome)
2. Preview: 3-5 key insights from the resource
3. Social proof: How many people already use it / results
4. Access CTA: "Comment [keyword]" or "Link in first comment"
5. Standard Footer

**Recommended Visual:** Carousel (Flat Cards showing resource preview)
