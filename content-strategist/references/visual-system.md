# Visual System — MMIND.ai Brand Design

## Color Palette (strict — no additional colors)

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Dusty Rose | #CC798E | Primary accent, headlines, key data |
| Soft Coral | #EE876F | Secondary accent, highlights, CTAs |
| Light Blush | #FAEAE9 | Backgrounds, cards, subtle fills |
| Light Gray | #F2F2F2 | Backgrounds, dividers, secondary elements |
| Near-Black | #0D0D0D | Text, lines, outlines |

**White (#FFFFFF)** is used as background in most layouts.

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Bold, clean sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica Neue, or similar)
- **Body:** Regular weight, same family
- **Data/Numbers:** Bold or semi-bold, oversized for emphasis
- **No serif fonts.** No handwritten or decorative fonts.

## 6 Visual Styles

### Stil A: Bold Data Statement
- **Format:** 4:5 Portrait (1080 × 1350 px)
- **Layout:** One large number/statistic + short context line
- **Colors:** White background, data in #CC798E or #EE876F, text in #0D0D0D
- **Use:** Awareness posts with striking data points
- **Example:** "95,4%" large, "der Kommunikationsprofis nutzen KI" below

### Stil B: Flat Cards Carousel
- **Format:** 4:5 Portrait (1080 × 1350 px per slide)
- **Layout:** Cards with rounded corners, one concept per card
- **Colors:** Card background #FAEAE9 or #F2F2F2, accent elements in #CC798E, text #0D0D0D
- **Use:** Education posts, step-by-step frameworks
- **Rules:** Max 8 slides. First slide = hook. Last slide = CTA. Consistent layout across slides.

### Stil C: Structured Infographic
- **Format:** 4:5 Portrait (1080 × 1350 px)
- **Layout:** Process flow, timeline, or comparison grid
- **Colors:** Light background, connection lines in #0D0D0D, nodes in #CC798E and #EE876F
- **Use:** How We Do It posts, methodology breakdowns

### Stil D: Data Comparison
- **Format:** 4:5 Portrait (1080 × 1350 px)
- **Layout:** Before/After or two-column comparison
- **Colors:** "Before" side muted (#F2F2F2), "After" side accent (#CC798E, #EE876F)
- **Use:** Conversion posts, case study results

### Stil E: Flat Timeline / Minimal Quote Card
- **Format:** 4:5 Portrait (1080 × 1350 px)
- **Layout:** Clean quote with attribution, or timeline with 3-5 milestones
- **Colors:** Minimal — white background, quote in #0D0D0D, accent line in #CC798E
- **Use:** Personal Story posts, milestone updates

### Stil F: Abstract Header
- **Format:** 16:9 Landscape (1280 × 720 px)
- **Layout:** One object, one idea, abstract geometric shapes
- **Colors:** White background, shapes in brand palette, outlines in #0D0D0D
- **Use:** Newsletter headers, blog headers, presentation visuals
- **Style references:** Stripe illustrations, Linear App graphics, Notion editorial art
- **Rule:** KEIN TEXT im Bild (title comes from the platform)

## Forbidden Elements

- No gradients
- No shadows or drop shadows
- No 3D effects
- No glassmorphism
- No photorealism
- No saturated colors outside the palette
- No stock photography aesthetics
- No text in header images (Stil F)
- No more than 5 colors in any single graphic

## Graphic Prompt Rules (for Nano Banana Pro / Gemini)

Every graphic prompt must be **complete and self-contained** — the AI image generator has no memory between prompts.

**Required elements in every prompt:**
1. Exact dimensions and orientation
2. Full color palette with hex codes
3. Style description (flat vector, editorial SaaS, etc.)
4. What to include
5. What to exclude (no text, no 3D, no gradients, etc.)
6. Style references (Stripe, Linear, Notion)
7. Optional: mmind.ai watermark, very small, bottom-right, #0D0D0D
